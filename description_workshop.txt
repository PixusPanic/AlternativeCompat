Unofficial Alternative Biomes Compatibility is a side-mod meant for both [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3150760112]Exxo Avalon Origins[/url], [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2882567428]The Depths[/url] and [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2601885797]The Confection[/url] (or just either) meant to help bridge compatibility between their custom alternative biomes (the Contagion, Depths, and Confection respectively) and a few other mods.
As such, it doesn't necessarily require either as a hard dependency, but it'll do nothing without any mod being enabled.

[url=https://discord.gg/SvdBYeSju3]Join my tModLoader Discord server here![/url]

-

Some examples of current compatibility:
- Baccilite (Avalon), Arquerite (Depths), and Neapolinite (Confection) storage tiers have been added for [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2563309347]Magic Storage[/url] users (with permission from yahooyowza for their sprites)
- Similarly, if MS is enabled, Bacterium Prime (Avalon) and Chasme (Depths) will also drop a Shadow Diamond upon being downed for the first time
- Modded gems will spawn in the Depths (currently works with Exxo Avalon Origins, Thorium Mod, and Verdant)
- If playing with [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2868085945]Better Potions[/url], the Crystal Skin Potion (Depths) will receive a slight buff if the Obsidian Skin Potion is also buffed
- [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2737642173]Mech Bosses Rework[/url]'s new mechs will drop Hallowed Ore or Neapolinite Ore (Confection) depending on the world evil (or both in drunk worlds)
